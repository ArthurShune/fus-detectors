\section{Bundle artifacts and naming conventions}
\label{app:bundle-schema}

This appendix records the artifact naming used in the released acceptance bundles and the mapping between the mathematical score notation in the main text and the stored arrays. These details are provided for reproducibility and are not required to follow the core derivations.
