\section{Bundle artifacts and naming conventions}
\label{app:bundle-schema}

This appendix summarizes the public bundle artifact categories and the mapping between the mathematical score notation in the main text and the stored arrays. Exact legacy compatibility filenames remain documented in the machine-readable reproducibility manifest; they are omitted here because they are not needed to follow the method or interpret the results.
